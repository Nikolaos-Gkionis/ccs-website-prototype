Delivering value for the public sector

Crown Commercial Service is the UK's largest public procurement organisation, working to maximise the value of public procurement:

  • Supporting over 17,000 public sector organisations
  • Managing commercial agreements worth billions of pounds
  • Delivering significant savings for the public sector
  • Promoting innovation and best practice in procurement
