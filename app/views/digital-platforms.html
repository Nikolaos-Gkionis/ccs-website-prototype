Digital platforms

Access our digital tools and platforms to manage your procurement activities, supplier relationships, and commercial agreements.

  • Contracts finder

    Search for government contracts and procurement opportunities across all public sector organizations.

  • Find a tender

    Discover current tender opportunities and procurement notices from government departments and public bodies.

  • Sign in to Purchasing Platform

    Access our purchasing platform to buy goods and services through our commercial agreements and frameworks.

  • Sign in to Digital Marketplace

    Access our digital marketplace to discover and procure digital services and technology solutions.

  • Sign in to fleet portal

    Access our fleet management portal to manage vehicle procurement, leasing, and fleet services.

  • Sign in to eMarketplace

    Access our electronic marketplace for streamlined procurement of goods and services through our frameworks.

  • Sign in to eSourcing

    Access your eSourcing account to view opportunities, submit bids, and manage your procurement activities.

  • eSourcing training

    Access training resources and guidance to help you use our eSourcing platform effectively and efficiently.
