Event: Interchange UK

About this event

Interchange is the UK’s only large scale integrated transport infrastructure platform. The event is dedicated to transforming and modernising transport infrastructure networks through integration, decarbonisation, and digitisation. Taking place at Manchester Central on 4 and 5 March 2025, Interchange has the active engagement and participation of all the key public and private infrastructure operators and their value chains offering unmatched opportunities to stimulate new ideas, challenge the orthodoxy and establish new relationships.
