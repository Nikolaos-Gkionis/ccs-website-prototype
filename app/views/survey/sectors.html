Sectors

In public procurement, one size doesn’t fit all. That’s why we have a team of specialist industry experts to help you achieve the best value for your organisation.

  • Local government

    We work with individual local authority colleagues, regional and national sector networks across the UK, as well as important stakeholders such as the Local Government Association and SOLACE to support the sector to achieve the best possible outcomes for their local communities through public procurement.

    Find out how we can help local government.

  • Health

    We work with all the different NHS organisations from individual NHS trusts and procurement groups, to Clinical Commissioning Groups, Integrated Care Systems, and other local partners to help you achieve your procurement goals.

    Find out how we can help the health sector.

  • Housing associations

    We want to help social housing providers save time and money when buying business goods and services, so that you can focus on the things that really matter to you and your tenants.

    Find out how we can help housing associations.

  • Schools and academies

    As the largest public procurement organisation in the UK, we’ve got a range of tailored solutions specifically for schools, academies and multi-academy trust.

    Find out how we can help schools and academies.

  • Central government and arm's length bodies

    Our team know that in order to improve efficiency and reduce duplication, there is a need to work more collaboratively, bringing together teams and sharing expertise across government departments, arm’s length bodies and the wider public sector.

    Find out how we can help central government.

  • Universities and colleges

    We have a dedicated team working with our higher and further education customers to help you achieve your procurement goals. Whether you’re a business manager responsible for buying office supplies, a finance manager looking to improve your payment processes, or an IT manager responsible for upgrading your networks and equipment.

    Find out how we can help universities and colleges.

  • Police and emergency services

    Police forces, fire and rescue services, ambulance trusts, coastguards and other emergency services have more important things to handle than administrative issues and costly purchases.

    Find out how we can help police and emergency services.

  • Charities

    We want to help charities and not for profit organisations save time and money when buying business goods and services, so that you can focus on making a real difference.

    Find out how we can help charities.

  • Devolved

    We work closely with strategic partners and collaborate with the Centres of Procurement Expertise across Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland to ensure our commercial agreements meet the needs of our customers in the devolved administrations by taking account of local policy and legislation.

    Find out how we can help devolved administrations.
