Your buying decisions have the power to create a fairer and more sustainable society

Our agreements have been designed to empower you to achieve the social value goals that are most important to your organisation. This includes:

  • diversifying your supply chains
  • reducing carbon emissions
  • promoting equality and diversity
  • making sure your supply chains are free from modern slavery

We're here to help you maximise social value in your procurement process.

How we can help you

  • What is social value?

    Learn about social value and how we maximise the social, environmental and economic benefits from every pound of public money spent.

    Find out more about social value.

  • Sustainable procurement

    We can help your organisation reduce its landfill waste, purchase sustainable cloud storage and limit the use of single-use plastics.

    Find out more about sustainable procurement.

  • Carbon net zero (CNZ)

    Our commercial agreements can help you meet CNZ objectives in everything from vehicle procurement to renewable energy.

    Find out more about carbon net zero.

  • Preventing modern slavery

    We have a zero-tolerance approach towards modern slavery and actively manage the risk in our commercial agreements.

    Find out more about preventing modern slavery.
